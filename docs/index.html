Cl-Ownpress

Cl-ownpress (hereby also referred as clown) is a personal publishing platform for the lisp hacker. It should help you gather content and publish it in a hacker friendly manner.

Architecture

Publishing with cl-ownpress happens in 2 phases, represented by its two primary modules.

  1. Providers
    Providers provide data to be published. Their job is to gather the data from wherever, and add it to an sqlite database.

  2. Publishers
    Publishers publish the content collected in the clown db.
